Casper Agentic Buildathon 2026 - Qualification Round
Activity Introduction
Welcome to the Casper Agentic Buildathon 2026 - Qualification Round, hosted by the Casper Association—a global online competition designed to attract, onboard, and empower the next generation of Web3/AI developers. Running from June 1 to June 30, 2026, with a hands-on developer workshop in Istanbul on June 2 during Istanbul Blockchain Week, this buildathon invites builders from all backgrounds to create production-ready applications at the convergence of Agentic AI, Decentralized Finance (DeFi), and Real-World Assets (RWA) on the Casper Network.

Positioning
The Casper Agentic Buildathon is open to everyone—solo hackers, seasoned teams, students, and entrepreneurs—who are passionate about building real, working applications powered by Agentic AI and deployed on Casper.

Overall Goal
Onboard developers to the Casper ecosystem at scale, accelerate the creation of usable, real-world dApps, position Casper at the intersection of DeFi, RWA, and AI innovation, demonstrate decentralized participation through community voting on the CSPR.fans app, and expand global awareness of Casper among builders, partners, and media.

Qualification Round Selection Mechanism
The Qualification Round uses a hybrid advancement mechanism:

Community Voting Path: The top 3 projects receiving the most votes via the CSPR.fans app advance directly to the Final Round without additional judging.

Builder Merit Path (Prototype-Based Qualification): All other projects must meet the technical eligibility criteria (working prototype on Casper Testnet with transaction-producing on-chain component) to advance to the Final Round for professional jury evaluation.

What is Agentic AI?
Agentic AI refers to autonomous AI agents that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions independently. In the context of this buildathon, participants will build agents that harness AI Agent Skills, Model Context Protocols (MCPs), and the X402 standard to enable autonomous agents to reliably connect wallets, sign transactions, manage assets, and interact with smart contracts on the Casper Network.

What are the example build directions?
Participants can build applications such as:

1. Autonomous Yield-Routing Agents via MCP Build an autonomous agent that actively monitors yield opportunities across Casper DeFi protocols. The agent uses Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers to expose Casper smart contract states to an AI LLM. The AI Agent Skill naively signs transactions and reallocates funds when yield thresholds are met, transforming a passive wallet into an active, self-driving portfolio manager.

2. RWA Oracle Agents with Verifiable On-Chain Identity Create an agent that scrapes off-chain data, runs a risk assessment model, and posts verified data on-chain via Casper's native X402 implementation (launching June 2026). The agent maintains a verifiable on-chain identity and reputation score based on historical accuracy, creating a trust-minimized RWA oracle.

3. Multi-Agent DAO Governance & Execution Design a system where a swarm of specialized agents (Risk Agent, Treasury Agent, Legal Agent) deliberate on DAO proposals and use the Casper SDK to autonomously execute transactions. This demonstrates autonomous coordination on-chain.

4. AI-Driven Compliance & KYC via Zero-Knowledge Build an agentic workflow handling KYC/AML compliance for DeFi protocols. The agent processes user documents off-chain, verifies them, and issues a compliance token. Using Casper's upgradable smart contracts, the agent can autonomously revoke or update compliance statuses based on continuous off-chain monitoring, without exposing underlying user data on-chain.
